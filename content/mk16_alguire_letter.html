Welcome to MKSAP 16 Digital!

We hope you enjoy using MKSAP 16 Digital, which encompasses MKSAP 16 Online as well as downloadable apps for tablets, desktops, and smartphones, including an app for Board Basics 3. Our goal in creating MKSAP 16 Digital was to accommodate your schedule and learning style by offering you access to MKSAP 16 content when you are at work, home, or traveling, whether you are connected to the Internet or not.

MKSAP 16 is the culmination of 2 years of effort by dozens of leading subspecialists across the United States. MKSAP 16's authoring committees have strived to help internists succeed in Maintenance of Certification and to help residents prepare for the certifying examination. MKSAP 16 also helps you update your medical knowledge with content in 11 subspecialty text sections and elevates standards of self-learning by allowing you to assess your knowledge with 1,200 all-new, statistically validated multiple-choice questions, which are among the best in medicine. To provide you with the freshest perspective possible, our authors, who are demonstrated leaders in internal medicine education, start from the ground up, writing completely new content each edition.

MKSAP 16 Digital's features and enhancements:

  • An interactive learning process, providing access to MKSAP 16's 11 text sections and 1,200 self-assessment questions
  • Links from questions to related text and vice versa, to reinforce your learning experience
  • Links to PubMed from MKSAP's bibliographic citations
  • Improved search functionality
  • Enhanced visual aids to document your learning progress
  • Same-day CME credit (also available for MKSAP 16 print users), with a personalized, frameable CME certificate
  • Improved customized quiz features, including exam (timed) and study (untimed) modes, expanded question-selection options, and hospitalist questionâ€“based quizzes to help you prepare for the Hospital Medicine Maintenance of Certification Examination
  • Special areas of focus, including
    • High-Value Care Recommendations. These concise recommendations, with links to associated self-assessment questions, focus on the important concept of balancing clinical benefit with costs and harms to address the needs of trainees, practicing physicians, and patients. High-Value Care recommendations are part of a major initiative that has been undertaken by the American College of Physicians, in collaboration with other organizations
    • Hospitalist-focused content. Specially featured hospitalist-focused text and questions address the learning needs of the increasing number of physicians who work in the hospital setting

MKSAP 16 Digital also lets you work offline from multiple locations. You can use the following:

  • Apps for iPads, Android-based tablets, and desktops (Macs and PCs), with access to the 11 text sections, 1,200 self-assessment questions, and Board Basics 3
  • Apps for iPhones or Android-based smartphones, with access to MKSAP 16's 1200 self-assessment questions and Board Basics 3

In the apps, you can view your progress and, while online, sync your answers from your apps to your MKSAP 16 Online account to keep your progress current. You will also benefit from easy access to MKSAP 16 Online for submission of same-day CME.
